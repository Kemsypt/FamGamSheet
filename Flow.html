Tevnyr: Mobile Striker / Utility
Raynor: Half-Orc Cleric / Tank

Tevnyr's job is not to stand still and trade hits. Her job is to strike distracted enemies, protect vulnerable allies, scout, steal, confuse, and escape before the enemy can properly respond.

Important Timing Rules

Back to top

Start here if you want to avoid accidental out-of-turn actions.

What Tevnyr can do on her own turn

  • Action: Attack, cast most spells, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Help, Hide, Use an Object, or use Mage Hand depending on the situation.
  • Bonus Action: Cunning Action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide. Misty Step is also a bonus action spell, so it uses the same bonus action slot.
  • Movement: Walk or fly up to her movement, split before and after actions if needed.
  • Free interaction: Draw a weapon, open an unlocked door, pick up one simple object, and similar small interactions.

What Tevnyr can do outside her own turn

  • Reaction only: Shield when hit by an attack, opportunity attack when an enemy leaves her reach, or other reaction-based effects if available.
  • She cannot cast Misty Step outside her turn unless a specific feature says she can.
  • She cannot Dash, Disengage, Hide, Attack, or cast normal action/bonus-action spells outside her turn.

Familiar timing

A familiar has its own place in initiative and acts on its own turn, but it cannot attack. It can still take actions like Help, Dash, Disengage, Dodge, Hide, or Search, depending on the situation.

  • The familiar can use Help on its turn to distract an enemy.
  • Then Tevnyr attacks on her turn and benefits if the Help effect still applies.
  • Ask the DM how they run familiar initiative, because tables sometimes simplify it.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

Mistake: “The enemy moves towards Halrick, so Tevnyr Misty Steps between them right now.”

Correct: Tevnyr can only Misty Step on her own turn. Outside her turn she usually only has reactions available.

Mistake: Standing beside enemies after attacking.

Correct: Rogues survive through movement, positioning, hiding, and escaping. Attack, then reposition whenever possible.

Mistake: Forgetting Sneak Attack.

Correct: Check every round whether Sneak Attack can trigger through allies, advantage, Faerie Fire, hiding, or familiar Help.

Mistake: Using Misty Step and then trying to Cunning Action Hide in the same turn.

Correct: Both use your bonus action. You only get one bonus action each turn.

Mistake: Sending the familiar into danger carelessly.

Correct: Familiars are fragile. Use them for scouting, Help actions, distractions, and information - not tanking damage.

Mistake: Flying in open air without cover.

Correct: Flying is strong, but being visible in the middle of the room makes Tevnyr an easy target for ranged enemies.

Mistake: Forgetting Mage Hand Legerdemain exists.

Correct: Tevnyr should constantly think about locks, levers, traps, stolen items, distractions, keys, potions, and remote interactions.

Mistake: Breaking Halrick's Area of Effect setup.

Correct: Do not drag enemies away from Halrick's planned spell area unless necessary.

Mistake: Splitting damage between too many enemies.

Correct: Help Kaimoira and Raynor finish enemies quickly. Dead enemies deal no damage.

Mistake: Forgetting Tevnyr is tiny.

Correct: Tiny size opens ridiculous possibilities - ceiling beams, tiny windows, gaps in walls, furniture, rafters, pipes, shelves, backpacks, and hiding spots other characters cannot use.

Party Battle Plan

Back to top

How Tevnyr works with Raynor, Kaimoira, and Halrick.

Raynor - Half-Orc Cleric / Tank

  • Holds the front line.
  • Creates reliable Sneak Attack targets.
  • Blocks doorways and choke points.
  • Gives Tevnyr a safe target to stab and leave.

Kaimoira - Elf Fighter / Ranged & Close Damage

  • Deals strong damage from range or up close.
  • Can switch between ranged pressure and melee support.
  • Works well with Faerie Fire.
  • Helps finish wounded priority targets.
  • Can hold a side lane while Tevnyr scouts or repositions.

Halrick - Human Wizard / Magic / Support / Area of Effect

  • Controls groups of enemies.
  • Benefits from Tevnyr creating distractions.
  • Needs protection from fast melee enemies.
  • Can punish enemies that bunch together near Raynor.

Tevnyr - Arcane Trickster Fairy

  • Flies, hides, scouts, and strikes.
  • Uses familiar and Faerie Fire to help trigger Sneak Attack.
  • Uses Mage Hand Legerdemain to steal, sabotage, and solve problems.
  • Uses spells to control, escape, or create openings.
Best overall rhythm: Raynor anchors the fight, Kaimoira deals heavy damage, Halrick shapes the battlefield, and Tevnyr strikes whoever is distracted, exposed, or threatening the back line.

Tevnyr's Turn Flow

Back to top

Use this during combat when deciding what Tevnyr should do next.

1. Start of Tevnyr's turn - choose the problem

  • Is Halrick threatened?
  • Is Raynor already beside an enemy?
  • Which target is Kaimoira focusing?
  • Can Tevnyr get Sneak Attack this turn?
  • Can Tevnyr end somewhere safer than where she started?

2. Pick a target

  1. Enemy threatening Halrick.
  2. Enemy locked in melee with Raynor.
  3. Enemy Kaimoira is already damaging.
  4. Enemy caster concentrating on a spell.
  5. Enemy isolated from the rest of the group.

3. Set up Sneak Attack

  • Attack a target beside Raynor.
  • Attack after the familiar used Help.
  • Attack a target affected by Faerie Fire.
  • Attack from hiding.
  • Attack with advantage from another source.
If Raynor is beside an enemy, that enemy is usually Tevnyr's cleanest Sneak Attack target.

4. Use Action

  • Attack if Sneak Attack is ready.
  • Green-Flame Blade if two enemies are close together.
  • Faerie Fire if several enemies are grouped and the party can benefit.
  • Tasha's Hideous Laughter if one dangerous enemy needs to be stopped.
  • Crown of Madness if enemies are clustered and chaos helps.
  • Minor Illusion to bait, distract, or hide something.
  • Mage Hand Legerdemain for theft, sabotage, doors, traps, or objects.

5. Use Bonus Action

  • Cunning Action: Hide after attacking from cover.
  • Cunning Action: Disengage before leaving melee.
  • Cunning Action: Dash to reach cover, height, or an objective.
  • Misty Step to teleport as a bonus action, but only on Tevnyr's own turn.
You only get one bonus action. If Tevnyr casts Misty Step, she cannot also bonus action Hide, Dash, or Disengage that same turn.

6. Movement and end position

  • Fly to a beam, balcony, tree branch, wall niche, ledge, bookshelf, or other awkward place.
  • Use tiny size to fit somewhere enemies cannot easily follow.
  • Do not block Kaimoira's line of sight.
  • Do not drag enemies towards Halrick.
  • Try to end near cover, above reach, or hidden.

Sneak Attack: Tevnyr's Most Important Combat Feature

Back to top

Use this section whenever you are unsure whether Tevnyr can add Sneak Attack damage.

Why Sneak Attack matters

Sneak Attack is Tevnyr's main damage engine. As a rogue, she is not built to make lots of attacks like a fighter or tank hits like Raynor. Instead, she wants one accurate, well-timed strike each round that deals heavy damage.

  • It makes Tevnyr's single attack matter.
  • It rewards smart positioning and teamwork.
  • It encourages attacking enemies distracted by Raynor.
  • It makes familiar Help, hiding, and Faerie Fire extremely valuable.
  • It lets Tevnyr contribute major damage while staying mobile and safe.
Simple rule of thumb: if Tevnyr attacks during combat, ask “Can this be a Sneak Attack?” before rolling damage.

Basic Sneak Attack checklist

Tevnyr can apply Sneak Attack if all of these are true:

  • She hits with a finesse or ranged weapon attack.
  • She does not have disadvantage on the attack roll.
  • She has advantage on the attack roll, or another enemy of the target is within 5 feet of it.
  • That nearby enemy of the target is not incapacitated.
  • Sneak Attack has not already been used this turn.
Important: Sneak Attack is once per turn, not once per round. Most of the time Tevnyr uses it on her own turn, but if she somehow makes a qualifying reaction attack on another creature's turn, it may be possible again.

Best ways Tevnyr can trigger Sneak Attack

  • Attack Raynor's target: Raynor is the tank, so enemies beside them are usually legal Sneak Attack targets.
  • Attack from hiding: If Tevnyr is hidden, she may attack with advantage.
  • Use familiar Help: The familiar uses Help on its turn, then Tevnyr attacks with advantage if the timing still works.
  • Use Faerie Fire: Enemies that fail the save can be attacked with advantage.
  • Attack restrained, prone, blinded, or otherwise vulnerable enemies: If the condition gives Tevnyr advantage, Sneak Attack may apply.
  • Coordinate with Kaimoira: Focus the same enemy so it drops faster.
  • Use Halrick's control spells: If Halrick makes enemies easier to hit, Tevnyr should capitalise.

Situations where Sneak Attack usually works

Enemy beside Raynor

Raynor is in melee with the enemy. Tevnyr shoots or stabs that enemy. This is the cleanest setup.

Familiar distracts the target

The familiar uses Help. Tevnyr attacks with advantage on her turn if the setup remains valid.

Tevnyr is hidden

Tevnyr hides behind cover, in rafters, behind furniture, or in darkness, then attacks from hiding.

Faerie Fire is active

An enemy fails against Faerie Fire. Tevnyr, Kaimoira, Raynor, and Halrick all benefit from easier targeting.

Enemy is focused on someone else

An enemy locked onto Raynor, Kaimoira, or Halrick may be exposed if Tevnyr has advantage or an ally is beside it.

Reaction attack opportunity

If an enemy leaves Tevnyr's reach and she makes an opportunity attack with a finesse weapon, Sneak Attack may apply if the conditions are met.

Situations where Sneak Attack might not work

  • Tevnyr has disadvantage on the attack roll.
  • The weapon is not ranged or finesse.
  • No ally is beside the target and Tevnyr does not have advantage.
  • The ally beside the target is incapacitated.
  • Tevnyr already used Sneak Attack on that same turn.
  • The familiar tried to Help, but the target moved or the timing no longer applies.
  • Tevnyr is hidden poorly and the DM rules the enemy can clearly see her.
Common mistake: Sneak Attack does not require Tevnyr to be sneaking. The name is misleading. It is really about striking a distracted or vulnerable enemy.

Sneak Attack Example 1 - Raynor Tank Setup

Raynor's turnRaynor moves into melee with a bugbear and holds the front line.

Tevnyr's turnTevnyr attacks the bugbear with a ranged or finesse weapon. Because Raynor is beside the bugbear, Sneak Attack can apply if Tevnyr hits and does not have disadvantage.

Raynor: “I've got its attention.”

Tevnyr: “Perfect. Keep looking big and heroic.”

Tevnyr: “I fire at the bugbear while it's busy with Raynor.”

Sneak Attack Example 2 - Familiar Help Setup

Familiar's turnThe familiar uses Help to distract a cultist.

Tevnyr's turnTevnyr attacks that cultist with advantage. If she hits, Sneak Attack applies.

Tevnyr: “Flit, make yourself deeply irritating.”

DM: “The familiar flutters in the cultist's face.”

Tevnyr: “Then I shoot while he's distracted.”

Sneak Attack Example 3 - Faerie Fire Team Setup

Tevnyr's turnTevnyr casts Faerie Fire on a cluster of enemies.

Later turnsEnemies that failed their saves grant advantage to attackers, helping Tevnyr trigger Sneak Attack and helping Kaimoira land more shots.

Tevnyr: “Glowing enemies are easier to stab. Science, probably.”

Kaimoira: “I like those odds.”

Sneak Attack Example 4 - Opportunity Attack Possibility

Enemy's turnAn enemy leaves Tevnyr's melee reach.

Tevnyr reactionTevnyr may make an opportunity attack if she has her reaction available.

DamageIf the attack qualifies, Sneak Attack can apply because this is a different turn from Tevnyr's own turn.

DM: “The cultist turns and runs past you.”

Tevnyr: “Rude. I use my reaction to stab him as he leaves.”

Ask the DM if the Sneak Attack conditions are met. This is legal only if Tevnyr has not used Sneak Attack on that enemy's turn already and the attack otherwise qualifies.

Scenario 1 - Classic Front Line Sneak Attack

Situation: Raynor is in melee with a hobgoblin. Kaimoira is attacking from the back. Halrick is preparing an Area of Effect if enemies bunch up.

Raynor's turnRaynor moves into the doorway and engages the hobgoblin.

Kaimoira's turnKaimoira attacks the hobgoblin, marking it as the priority target.

Halrick's turnHalrick holds position and warns the party not to scatter enemies away from the doorway.

Tevnyr's turnTevnyr flies from behind cover, attacks the hobgoblin because it is beside Raynor, applies Sneak Attack, then uses Bonus Action Disengage or Hide depending on whether she is still threatened.

Raynor: “I've got this one held.”

Kaimoira: “Same target. Bring it down.”

Tevnyr: “Easy. Keep it looking at you.”

Tevnyr: “I swoop in, stab while it's busy with Raynor, then duck behind the broken pillar.”

Why it works: Tevnyr's Sneak Attack is legal because an ally is within 5 feet of the target, and her bonus action happens on her own turn.

Scenario 2 - Enemy Threatens the Halrick

Situation: A fast enemy moves towards Halrick. It is currently the enemy's turn, not Tevnyr's.

Enemy's turnThe enemy moves towards Halrick and attacks Halrick if it can reach.

Tevnyr outside her turnTevnyr cannot Misty Step, Dash, Disengage, or cast Tasha's Hideous Laughter right now. She can only use a reaction if a trigger applies. For example, Shield only works if Tevnyr herself is hit.

Tevnyr's next turnNow Tevnyr can act. She can fly or Misty Step into position, attack the enemy, cast Tasha's Hideous Laughter, use Minor Illusion to distract, or help Halrick escape by pressuring the threat.

DM: “The ghoul rushes past the front line towards Halrick.”

Tevnyr: “Can I Misty Step in front of it?”

DM: “Not until your turn. It is the ghoul's turn right now.”

Tevnyr: “Got it. On my turn, I Misty Step onto the desk beside Halrick and cast Tasha's Hideous Laughter on the ghoul.”

Why it works: Misty Step and Tasha's Hideous Laughter are used on Tevnyr's own turn, not as an interrupt.

Scenario 3 - Familiar Help Into Sneak Attack

Situation: Tevnyr's familiar is in initiative before Tevnyr.

Familiar's turnThe familiar flies to the enemy and uses the Help action to distract it.

Enemy's turnIf the enemy acts before Tevnyr, the plan might fail if it moves away, attacks the familiar, or changes position.

Tevnyr's turnIf the Help still applies, Tevnyr attacks with advantage and can apply Sneak Attack if she hits.

Tevnyr: “Flit, be a nuisance.”

DM: “On Flit's turn, it swoops around the bandit's face and uses Help.”

Tevnyr: “When my turn comes up, I fire from behind the statue with advantage.”

Why it works: The familiar acts on its own turn. Tevnyr receives the benefit later if the timing still lines up.

Scenario 4 - Faerie Fire Team Setup

Situation: Three enemies are clustered near Raynor. Kaimoira has line of sight. Halrick wants easier targets for follow-up spells.

Tevnyr's turnTevnyr uses her action to cast Faerie Fire on the group. She then uses movement to fly behind cover. She may use Cunning Action Hide if she has not used her bonus action for something else and the DM agrees she has a place to hide.

Kaimoira's turnKaimoira attacks a glowing enemy with advantage if the enemy failed its save, whether fighting at range or up close.

Raynor's turnRaynor attacks or holds the front line against glowing enemies.

Halrick's turnHalrick can target the highlighted enemies more confidently, especially if invisibility was an issue.

Tevnyr: “They're all nicely packed together. Rude not to light them up.”

Tevnyr: “I cast Faerie Fire over the cluster near Raynor, then fly behind the archway.”

Kaimoira: “Perfect. I shoot the glowing one.”

Why it works: Faerie Fire uses Tevnyr's action on her own turn and helps the whole party afterwards.

Scenario 5 - Green-Flame Blade Near the Tank

Situation: Two enemies are beside Raynor. Tevnyr can reach one safely.

Tevnyr's turnTevnyr flies or moves into melee, casts Green-Flame Blade as her action, hits one enemy, and the flame jumps to the second if conditions are met.

Same turnIf Tevnyr is now in melee and needs to escape, she can use Bonus Action Disengage with Cunning Action, then fly away.

Raynor: “Two on me!”

Tevnyr: “That sounds like a them problem.”

Tevnyr: “I fly in, use Green-Flame Blade on the closest one, then bonus action Disengage and get back above them.”

Why it works: Green-Flame Blade is Tevnyr's action. Disengage is her bonus action. Both happen on her own turn.

Scenario 6 - Shield as a Proper Reaction

Situation: An archer shoots Tevnyr between her turns.

Enemy's turnThe enemy attacks Tevnyr and the attack hits.

Tevnyr reactionTevnyr casts Shield, increasing her AC until the start of her next turn. If the new AC is high enough, the attack misses.

Tevnyr's next turnTevnyr can now reposition, hide, retaliate, or fly behind better cover.

DM: “The archer hits Tevnyr with a 17.”

Tevnyr: “No thanks. I cast Shield.”

DM: “The arrow flashes off a magical barrier.”

Why it works: Shield is a reaction spell with a specific trigger: Tevnyr is hit by an attack.

Scenario 7 - Halrick Area of Effect Setup Without Breaking Timing

Situation: Halrick wants enemies grouped for an Area of Effect next round.

Tevnyr's turnTevnyr uses Minor Illusion to create the sound of reinforcements coming from one side passage, then flies back towards Raynor's defensive line.

Enemies' turnsIf fooled, enemies may move away from the fake sound and towards Raynor or doorway.

Halrick's turnHalrick casts an Area of Effect spell on the newly grouped enemies.

Halrick: “If they gather by the door, I can hit all of them.”

Tevnyr: “Right. I'll make the other hallway sound worse.”

Tevnyr: “On my turn, I cast Minor Illusion: heavy boots and shouting from the side passage. Then I fly back behind Raynor.”

Why it works: Tevnyr creates the bait on her turn. Enemies react on their own turns. Halrick acts on Halrick's turn.

Scenario 8 - Mage Hand Legerdemain During Combat

Situation: A cultist has a key on their belt and is standing near a locked cage.

Tevnyr's turnTevnyr uses Mage Hand Legerdemain to attempt to lift the key or manipulate the lock from a distance, depending on what the DM allows and how the hand is already positioned.

Party follow-upKaimoira covers or engages the cultist, Raynor blocks enemies from reaching the cage, and Halrick prepares control magic if the prisoners are freed.

Tevnyr: “While everyone's focused on Raynor, my invisible Mage Hand goes for the key.”

DM: “Roll Sleight of Hand.”

Tevnyr: “With pleasure.”

Why it works: Tevnyr is using an action on her turn to manipulate the scene, not interrupting another creature's turn.

Outside Combat Flow

Back to top

Use this for exploration, infiltration, scouting, and problem solving.

1. Scout first

  • Send the familiar through windows, rafters, vents, tree lines, alleys, or over walls.
  • Use Tevnyr's tiny size to slip into places the others cannot reach.
  • Fly up to inspect balconies, rooftops, ledges, and guard towers.
  • Return with information before Raynor commits the party to a risky entrance.

2. Use the party properly

  • Raynor: Best as the confident front-facing presence and physical protector.
  • Kaimoira: Best for tracking, outdoor scouting, watching exits, and covering from range or stepping into melee.
  • Halrick: Best for magic, research, ritual thinking, and interpreting arcane threats.
  • Tevnyr: Best for sneaking, unlocking, stealing, distracting, lying, improvising, and getting into tiny places.

3. Use spells before trouble starts

  • Disguise Self: Enter a restricted area before the alarm is raised.
  • Charm Person: Smooth over a conversation before it becomes a fight.
  • Minor Illusion: Pull guards away from a route.
  • Mage Hand Legerdemain: Unlock, steal, plant, or trigger things safely.
  • Find Familiar: Scout first so Tevnyr does not have to guess.
  • Druidcraft: Add flavour, signals, subtle distractions, or ominous fairy weirdness.

Quick Rules Reference

Back to top

Fast reminders for the table.

On Tevnyr's turn

  • Move or fly.
  • Take one action.
  • Take one bonus action if available.
  • Use one free object interaction if reasonable.

Outside Tevnyr's turn

  • Usually only reactions.
  • Shield only when Tevnyr is hit.
  • No Misty Step unless it is her turn.
  • No Cunning Action unless it is her turn.

Familiar

  • Acts on its own turn.
  • Cannot attack.
  • Can Help, scout, distract, or watch.
  • Works best when its turn happens before Tevnyr's.

Sneak Attack setup

  • Enemy beside Raynor.
  • Tevnyr has advantage.
  • Familiar Help applies.
  • Faerie Fire applies.
  • Tevnyr attacks from hiding.

Sneak Attack

  • Use once per turn.
  • Needs finesse or ranged weapon.
  • Works with advantage or ally beside target.
  • Check it every time Tevnyr hits.
